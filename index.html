About GreenGrease Solutions

GreenGrease Solutions provides expert collection of used cooking oil and grease waste from commercial kitchens in Tacoma and nearby areas. We help businesses keep kitchens safe and clean by handling oil removal efficiently and recycling responsibly to protect the environment.

Founded with a commitment to sustainability and excellent service, our team brings years of experience in waste management and environmental services to every job.

We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients by providing reliable, professional service that meets all regulatory requirements while minimizing environmental impact.
